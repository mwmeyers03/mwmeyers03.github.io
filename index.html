// 01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Matthew Meyers, an Electrical Engineering & Biomedical Engineering student at the University of Central Florida. I'm passionate about technology and the marriage of the medical and engineering fields.

My work spans PCB design, signal processing, machine learning, and robotics. I thrive on interdisciplinary challenges — from laying out boards for digital audio systems to building ML pipelines that work with real-world acoustic data.

As an active member of IEEE UCF, I've contributed to projects that tackle real-world problems, including a pandemic-era surface-disinfection robot funded by an IEEE Region 3 grant.
